You've come to the perfect place if you're thinking about pursuing a nursing career in England. With nurses from all over the world working with us, we have one of the most diversified workforces in the world. Joining our dedicated team of nurses will allow you to benefit from employment stability, a competitive income, and flexible hours.

No upfront recruitment expenses are required, and we'll give you a thorough induction to make you feel at home. Along with giving you a welcome package and any information you might require, we'll help you till you feel at home.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Manage the nursing care for all patients during your shift
  • Recognize changes in patients’ condition and respond appropriately
  • Scheduling procedures and operations if necessary
  • Managing their medications and work with other healthcare staff and doctors to ensure they are updated about the patients care and needs.
  • Ensuring doctors are informed about the patients’ health history in order to accurately observe and record the patient’s condition and behaviour.
  • Checking and ordering supplies

The Sterling Staffing team offers highly qualified healthcare personnel on demand to clients like care facilities, clinics, and supported living services.
